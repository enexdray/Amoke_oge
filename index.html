Our Story

From Our Kitchen to Your Home

Founded in 2010, Amoke Oge Restaurant has been serving authentic Yoruba dishes with recipes passed down through generations.

With over 100 dedicated staff members, we take pride in preparing each meal with the same care and attention as we would for our own families.

Our Mission

To preserve and share the rich culinary heritage of Yoruba culture while providing exceptional service and quality to our customers.
